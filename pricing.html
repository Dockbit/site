Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I use Dockbit for free?

    Yes. Our Free plan gives your team 2 pipelines with 100 deployments, unlimited users and unlimited integrations for free.

  • How does the 30-day trial work?

    After you sign up for your trial, you'll get access to all features of the chosen plan for 30 days. You don't have to provide your credit card information at this point, since we'll notify you 3 days prior to the trial period end. If you need to more time to explore Dockbit, feel free to contact us and we'll be glad to extend your trial period.

  • What happens at the end of my trial?

    At the end of your trial period, we'll attempt to charge the cost of the chosen plan. If you haven't provided your credit card information, we'll automatically downgrade your account to Free plan and you won't be able to access any of the paid features.

  • Can I change or cancel my plan at any time?

    Absolutely. You can change or cancel your plan anytime. If you downgrade your plan, your data will remain intact. However, you won't be able to access it until you upgrade to the same or higher plan. If you cancel your plan, we'll not charge your credit card for the next period and your account will expire.

  • What if I need more pipelines?

    If you need more pipelines in your plan, please contact us and we'll be more than happy to provide you with a more advanced plan.

  • What is a Docker-enabled pipeline?

    Docker-enabled pipeline defines the maximum number of pipelines that can include Docker integrations to build and publish Docker images. Free plan utilizes small worker type, whereas Basic and Plus, medium and large, respectively. If you need more resources, let us know.

  • Do you provide on-premises version of Dockbit?

    We don't yet provide on-premises version of the Dockbit, however, contact us and we'd be glad to discuss your needs and work towards the solution for your team.

Need more advanced plan or have questions? Contact us.
